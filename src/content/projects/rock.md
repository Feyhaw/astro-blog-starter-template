abstract: | 
    Following other documentaries made in previous years, this time the stage will be dedicated to rock music. From the 1950s to the end of the Cold War (did it really end?), Rock music has come a long way: R&B, proto-rock, classic rock, punk, and not to mention the country, outlaw, and gospel. Beginning as my personal interest and a small detour from my research on polar architecture, I came across the amazing history of rock music. Like the KFC, it also reflects the struggles, culture, and the site conditions that created polar architecture in the first place. It is impossible to review the entire history of Rock and Roll; this document will trace the emergence and decline of classic rock and how it was shaped by the Cold War.
