abstract: | 
    As a homemade air conditioner, AirconF can produce a temperature difference of 3 Celsius between the environment and its exhaust. The cooling effect is dramatic, if not psychological. The main issue is the fan's low power. With a higher rpm of the out-fan, the device may produce some cooler wind. Further experiments with more powerful fans (like desktop fans) can be expected if the heatwave continues. In general, I will not say it is a serious experiment or pretend to be some sort of authority on homemade air conditioners; it is simply an interesting activity and a good learning experience.
