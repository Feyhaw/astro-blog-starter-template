description: A reconstruction of several typical labyrinth with parametric design tools 

abstract: | 
    As the explorations and experiments are carried out, the unicursal mazes are not always expandable due to a lack of persistent geometric and parametric recurrence. For expandable unicursal mazes, there are two fundamental approaches to enlarging the geometry: parametric expansion of the path and parametric expansion of the walls.
    
    For each expandable unicursal maze, there are shapes and numerical arrangements that can be used to read the maze's formation. The parametric design of the mazes depends on the parametric deciphering of them; it is important to design the algorithm to solve the maze at first, and the way of unveiling the underlying law may create bias.

    But isn’t our way of reading the world biased after all?
