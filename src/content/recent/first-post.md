<h3>This is my first post!</h3>

<p>
Welcome to my personal website! This little site began on a hot June day two weeks
ago. The heat wave came and receded, now it's hot again - more reason to study polar
architecturs perhaps? 
</p>

<p>
As you may have read in the manual aready, this site is expected to incorporate AI 
real-time discussions in the future. Currently, I don't have enough fund to support
the operation of the API. Therefore, I and AI have to design a cataloguing system 
to retrieve and associate different entries under the name of "librarian" - which 
was initially reserved for real AI. So, there is a working librarian. It is hardly 
a smart one, but I reckcon that it is still a decent first step.
</p>  

<p>
Having said that, it surprises me how far I have gone since the moment I put down 
my first line of code. Inintially, this website meant to be a portfolio to showcase 
my work and art. Then it evolved into something more than. With the dynamic background,
it started to breath. It started to resemble a map with the navigator on the edge of 
the main pages. Now it even serve to organize my previous projects. It is now an online 
archive. There are traces from the erlier days though; for example, vistors still have 
access to my cv. 
</p>

<p>
Now Caspsule Fey beta version is online - with still a lot to do. These days I'm also 
working on my Biosphere 2 article; one lesson I learned from that project is to get 
things going. There is always much more to learn, but there's not always so much time, 
as the world will never wait for you. The journey of Capsule Fey has only begun, and I
am very happen to greet you from this tiny corner of the internet.
</p>
